About Thunder

15 Years Old | Egyptian Developer from Dakahlia

Hello World! My name is Ahmed Al-Bahrawy, but you can call me "Thunder". I started my coding journey at the age of 12, initially learning game development with Unity Engine. Since then, I've evolved into a full-stack developer with a passion for backend systems.

I'm mostly self-taught, with additional training from Digital Cubs of Egypt Initiative, ITI (Information Technology Institute), and NTI (National Telecommunication Institute). I love logical thinking and calculations, which is why I prefer backend development over frontend.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing chess, reading Arabic and translated novels, and working on my language skills. My English is good, and I aspire to master German alongside my native Arabic.
